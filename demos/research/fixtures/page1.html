Acme Robotics launches Polaris, a turnkey assembly cell for SMB manufacturers

By Riya Patel - April 22, 2026

Today we are shipping Polaris, our first end-to-end assembly cell designed for shops that have outgrown manual fixturing but cannot justify a full automation line. Polaris pairs a 6-axis cobot, a dual-camera vision stack, and our new Aim path planner in a single compact footprint.

Why it matters

In customer pilots over the last six months, Polaris cut changeover time from 45 minutes to 6 minutes for medium-mix runs. The cell self-calibrates from a printed reference frame, and Aim produces collision-free trajectories in under 200 ms even on the embedded controller.

Pricing and availability

Polaris starts at $128,500 and ships in Q3 2026 in North America. European certification is targeted for Q4 2026. We are accepting deposits today.
