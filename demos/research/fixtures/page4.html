Driftwave welcomes Lena Park as Chief Financial Officer

By the Driftwave Team - May 6, 2026

Lena Park has joined Driftwave as Chief Financial Officer. Lena most recently led financial planning at a leading payments company and brings experience scaling finance teams through the IPO milestone.

Welcome, Lena

"Driftwave is at the inflection point I love: strong product, real revenue, and an enormous market still to be educated. I am excited to help the team scale the next chapter responsibly." - Lena Park

Lena will partner with the CEO and COO to evolve pricing strategy, open enterprise procurement motions, and prepare Driftwave for the next round of growth investment. She is based in New York.

Hiring

Beyond Lena's arrival, Driftwave has 22 open roles across product, engineering, finance, and go-to-market.
