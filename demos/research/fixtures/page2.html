BlueLeaf raises $40M Series B led by Northwind Capital to expand carbon accounting

By Marco Tan - April 29, 2026

BlueLeaf has closed a $40M Series B led by Northwind Capital, with participation from Greenfield Ventures and Sandhill Partners. The round brings total funding to $62M.

How funds will be used

  • Triple scope-3 emissions data coverage by ingesting supplier APIs from major ERP systems.
  • Ship an auditor-ready export module aligned with the 2026 CSRD reporting cycle.
  • Open a London office for the growing EU customer base.

Customer momentum

ARR grew 4.1x over the last twelve months. BlueLeaf now serves 380 enterprise accounts, including three Fortune 100 manufacturers. Net revenue retention is 142%, and the company is hiring 35 people across product, engineering, and customer success.
