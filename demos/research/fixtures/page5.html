Ember Labs releases Spark 1.5 with offline-first sync and attachment redaction

By Hugo Lefevre - May 7, 2026

Spark 1.5 is out today across web, macOS, Windows, iOS, and Android. The release focuses on working anywhere, privacy by default, and meeting users in the apps they already use.

Offline-first sync

Every Spark workspace now keeps a local CRDT-backed mirror. Users can read, write, comment, and resolve threads with no network. Changes converge automatically when connectivity returns.

Attachment redaction

Image and PDF uploads now offer one-click redaction. Faces, license plates, and email addresses are detected client-side, and the original file never leaves the device unredacted.

Pricing and rollout

There is no price change. Spark 1.5 rolls out over the next 48 hours, and admins can opt into early access from workspace settings.
